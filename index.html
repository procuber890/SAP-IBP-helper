What is SAP IBP?

SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP) is a cloud-based software tool used by companies to manage their supply chain and business planning. It helps businesses predict demand, plan production, optimize inventory, and respond to disruptions in real-time.

Why is IBP important?

Companies need to stay flexible and efficient. SAP IBP allows you to:

  • Forecast customer demand using real data.
  • Match supply to demand by planning production and procurement.
  • Manage inventory to avoid overstocking or running out.
  • Respond faster to changes in market, suppliers, or production delays.

IBP connects data from different departments — sales, production, inventory, and finance — to help companies make smarter decisions.
