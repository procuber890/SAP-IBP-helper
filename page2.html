Main Modules

  • IBP for Demand — Forecasting customer needs using data analysis and machine learning.
  • IBP for Supply — Matching available materials and factory capacity to the forecasted demand.
  • IBP for Inventory — Calculating safety stock and planning inventory levels globally.
  • Response and Supply Planning — Reacting to supply chain changes, like late shipments or production delays, in real time.
  • Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP) — Aligning company-wide planning across departments into one consistent plan.

Summary:

SAP IBP gives you tools to simulate scenarios, optimize supply chains, and predict risks before they happen. This allows companies to avoid shortages, reduce waste, and keep customers happy.
